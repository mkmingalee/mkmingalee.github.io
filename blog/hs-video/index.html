Promotion Video for Hanssem Rehaus Department

November 30th, 2016

Hanssem REHAUS Department is a department that distributes and sells interior products such as marble, floor, windows, and kitchen furnitures. Style package is an interior remodeling service product that combines products sold by the REHAUS department and sells them in style and also carries out construction. We made a promotional video using Time Labs to promote these products and the department.

  • Client | HANSSEM REHUAS department
  • Cowork | Minkyoung Lee (director), Jaewon Song (art director), Woori Nam (copyrighter), Seungjae Lee (camera director)
  • Duration | 2 months
  • Responsibility | project managemenr, managing shooting field, video planning, scenario, graphic design inspection
  • Tools | Illustrator, Photoshop, Premiere Pro

We used the department's logo as a loading bar to show how much interior construction in the video has progressed to maximize the promotional effect and reveal where this video is came from.

Worked By | Minkyoung Lee, Studio K110
