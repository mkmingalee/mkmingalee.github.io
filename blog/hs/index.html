Catalog Design for Hanssem Rehaus Department

July 29th, 2016

HANSSEM Rehaus Department's catalog design reorganization and Rehaus exhibition nameplate design. As Hanssem IK Department was reorganized as Rehaus Despartment, I commissioned a new design strucure for the department's catalog and the nameplate design of exhibition hall to improve the image of the division.

CHALLENGE

The previous booklet felt more like a guide than a design catalog. In order to reduce the feeling of stiffness and distraction, I arranged the concept of each interior design styles. For unity, image sources such as 3D rendering images and style board images in the booklet were also created.

Rendered images of the interior were produced to give the impression of fashion magazines, and photos of the products were included in the appendix at the back of the book away from the rendered images.

To help users easily understand the characteristics of each style, I created a board showing the style and concept.
