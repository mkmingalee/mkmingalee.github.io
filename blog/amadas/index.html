Identity Design for Smart Doorlock Company, Amadas

March 18th, 2017

I made a logo for Smart Doorlock Company, Amadas, by making an exclusive font for them. It is based on a font 'Optima' to express Amadas's delicate and the integrative nature of art and technology. It was developed by adding a curved surface following the motif from the shape of the handle to the basic solidity and structure of the font Optima.

The existing logo was too rigid and masculine, so it did not go well with the company's flexible form.

Moreover, you must walk like a camel, which is said to be the only beast which ruminates when walking.
