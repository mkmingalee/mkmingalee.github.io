Identity Design for Information Sceince and Culture Department

June 30th, 2015

This is an identity design and booklet design for the Department of Information Science and Culture, combined major of Seoul National University. Designed with the keyword ISC, which is an abbreviation of Information Science and Culture, it was to express the Digit style by taking advantage of the characteristics of the department that mainly deals with the understanding of IT-based industry and contents production.

  • Client | Information Sceince and Culture Department at the Seoul National University
  • Duration | 2 months
  • Responsibility | project management, catalog composition, design contents production, editing design, print supervision
  • Tools | Indesign, Illustrator, Photoshop
