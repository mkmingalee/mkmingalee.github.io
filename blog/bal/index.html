Identity Design for Fund Raising Campaign 2015, Seoul National University

February 27th, 2015

The Seoul National University Fund Raising Department is an institution that manages and awards scholarships. I participated as a senior researcher in the development of a design to be used for events and campaigns to be held in 2015.

  • Client | Seoul National University Fund Raising Development
  • Cowork | Minkyoung Lee (senior researcher), Prof. Kymn Kyungsun (directoer), Sohee Kim (researcher), Hye-eun Lim (researcher)
  • Duration | 3 months
  • Responsibility | project management, design planning, graphic production
  • Tools | Indesign, Illustrator, Photoshop

The design is based on the Seoul National University's signature triangle shape and the drawing of the university's tree "zelkova leaves".

2015 Scholarship Award Ceremony

The colors are blue, which symbolizes goodness and sincerity, and yellow green, which symbolizes the freshness of zelkova trees. In addition to the logo, we developed several design applications to be used for 2015 Seoul National University Fund Raising Canpaign.
Applications are used in February 2015 Scholarship Award Ceremony of Seoul National University.
